Introduce

History

It is a fruit and vegetable company that supplies fresh fruits and vegetables produced through quality control to major markets. We have built a unified system in packaging, transportation and sales through more than 40 production bases around the Europe to supply more than 100 of the highest quality products. TROPICO has enriched the table by significantly increasing the variety of new imported fruits such as bananas, gold pineapples, grapes, oranges, and mangoes. The diversification of these products can be said to be the result of strict quality inspection and improvement of the cold chain that is transported at the correct time.

TROPICO's story

2008

TROPICO brand introduced to consumers for the first time.

2009

First TROPICO logo appeared

2011

California Packing Corporation (CalPaK), the predecessor of TROPICO, imposed strict quality on its premium brand.

2013

The first food processing company in the United States to attach nutritional labels to its products.

2014

Launched a new variety, TROPICO Gold® Extra Sweet pineapple, developed after years of research

2018

Acquisition of Fresh Produce to establish a bridgehead for entering the European, African markets for processed fruits and vegetables and beverages

2020

The 12th anniversary of the birth of the TROPICO brand-a global brand that has been with consumers for the past 12 years since its debut in 2002

news

Forella pears are a petit, bell-shaped pear with yellow skin that is dotted with crimson red freckles (known as lenticels) signaling the pears' maturity. Younger Forella pears will be green while the red coloring is always present. Only ripe Forella pears will possess qualities that highlight the pears best virtues.
